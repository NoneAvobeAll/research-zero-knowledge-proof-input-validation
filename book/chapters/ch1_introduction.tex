\chapter{Introduction and Motivation}

This chapter motivates the need for zero-knowledge-proof-backed input validation
in web applications, with a focus on sensitive domains such as healthcare forms.
It introduces the high-level problem, outlines shortcomings of conventional
validation pipelines, and presents the research question driving PVE-ZKP.
