\chapter{PVE-ZKP Architecture}

This chapter presents the browser--validator--receiver architecture implemented
in the PVE-ZKP prototype. It describes the message flows between the browser
client, the validation service on port 3001, and the receiving system on port
3002, and explains how proof identifiers (proofId) decouple validation from the
final acceptance decision.
